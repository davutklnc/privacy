MealFlow Support

Last updated May 20, 2026

If you need help with MealFlow, contact support at support@lingotales.io.

What to Include

To help us understand the issue, please include:

  • A short description of what happened.
  • The steps that led to the issue.
  • Your device model and iOS version.
  • The MealFlow app version, if available.
  • Screenshots or screen recordings, if they help explain the issue.

Common Help Topics

iCloud Sync

MealFlow sync and sharing depend on iCloud availability, your Apple ID, iCloud Drive, network access, and device settings. If sync does not appear to work, check that you are signed into iCloud and that iCloud is enabled for MealFlow.

Shared Shopping Lists

If shared lists are not updating, confirm that everyone is signed into iCloud, has accepted the share invitation, and has network access. Apple controls iCloud sharing invitations and account-level permissions.

Recipe Importing

Some websites block extraction or format recipes in ways that are difficult to read automatically. If an imported recipe is incomplete, edit it manually after import.

Subscriptions and Purchases

If MealFlow offers paid features, purchases are managed by Apple. You can view, cancel, or change subscriptions in your Apple ID settings. Refund requests are handled by Apple through the App Store.
